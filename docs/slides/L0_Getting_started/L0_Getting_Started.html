Course Syllabus

Please read the syllabus.

The most current version of the syllabus is located on the course website.

Basic overview

15 weeks in the semester

12 Learning module weeks

3 exam weeks (2 midterms, one final)

Schedule is posted on the syllabus

Assessments and grading

There are 12 learning modules, and three exams. The grading rubric is below:

Assignment Points Total
12 Weekly Modules 5+ up to 60
Extra Credit Over 60/5 up to 10
Midterm 1 13.33 13.33
Midterm 2 13.33 13.33
Final Exam 13.33 13.33
100

Weekly learning modules

  1. Posted weekly (or earlier) on Blackboard and Course Website
  2. Include readings, mini-lectures, and quizzes/assignments
  3. Complete the work within the week (submit by due date)
  4. Assignment choice (see next slide)

Assignment choice

  • modules have multiple possible assignments, (e.g., quiz and writing assignment).

  • you choose which assignments to complete.

  • Complete as many as you want, max 60 points toward final grade

  • If you miss an assignment that is OK, you can do an extra one in a future module

Extra-credit

  • If you complete more than 60 points of assignments/quizzes, then your extra points will be converted to extra credit points ta a ratio of 5 to 1.

  • 100 - 60 = 40/5 = 8 extra credit points

  • maximum 10 extra credit points

Exams

  • There are three exams, 13.33 points each

  • Exams will be open for a whole week

  • Exams are multiple choice, around 45+ questions, test content from preceding learning modules.

  • The questions are similar to the quiz questions. Quizzes are practice for the exams.

Academic Integrity

It is your responsibility to understand the CUNY academic integrity policy and conduct your behavior according to a high standard of academic integrity.
